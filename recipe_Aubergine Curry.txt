Aubergine and Tomato Curry

Ingredients

* 2 aubergines
* 3 tomatoes
* 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds
* 1/2 teaspoon of black mustard seeds
* 1/2 teaspoon of tumeric powder
* 1 teaspoon of salt
* 4 garlic cloves
* 2 chillis

Preperation

1. Preheat the grill to high. Cut each aubergine in half, place on oven tray for 15-20 minutes until skin is black and tender
2. Remove tray from oven, carefully remove skin from aubergine and place the remains in a bowl.
3. Add a little oil to a saucepan on medium heat, add 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds, 1/2 teaspoon of black mustard seeds, 2 finely chopped garlic cloves and 2 sliced green chillis.
4. Slice tomatoes in small pieces, add 1/2 teaspoon of tumeric and 1 teaspoon of salt - simmer for 3-5 minutes.
5. Peel and dice the remaining aubergine, add to the saucepan and continue to cook for 8-10 mins
