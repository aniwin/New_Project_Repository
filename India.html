Aubergine Curry

Aubergine Curry is an authentic, south Indian curry which is an all around staple dish.It is healthy, simple and a delight to make.

  • Ingredients
  • Equipment
  • Preparation
  • Cooking

Ingredients
  • 2 aubergines
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon of tumeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 chillis

Equipment
  • An oven tray
  • A saucepan

Preparation
  1. Preheat the grill to high. Cut each aubergine in half, place on oven tray for 15-20 minutes until skin is black and tender
  2. tray from oven, carefully remove skin from aubergine and place the remains in a bowl.
  3. Add a little oil to a saucepan on medium heat, add 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds, 1/2 teaspoon of black mustard seeds, 2 finely chopped garlic cloves and 2 sliced green chillis.
  4. lice tomatoes in small pieces, add 1/2 teaspoon of tumeric and 1 teaspoon of salt - simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Peel and dice the remaining aubergine, add to the saucepan and continue to cook and simmer for 8-10 mins

Cooking
  • Cook until soft, option to add coriander to the top. Serve with rice or chappati
